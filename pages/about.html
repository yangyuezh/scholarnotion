About ScholarNotion

ScholarNotion is a data-first publication model inspired by OWID.

Editorial principles

  • Every claim links to source data.
  • Charts include transparent definitions and caveats.
  • Updates preserve version history.

Product architecture

  • Topics: thematic knowledge hubs.
  • Charts: reusable indicator pages.
  • Insights: short, frequent evidence-based analyses.
