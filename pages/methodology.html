Methodology

Transparency rules for data sourcing, transformation, and chart publication.

Data source policy

  • Prefer official statistical agencies, peer-reviewed datasets, or international institutions.
  • Store source links and snapshot dates for every update.
  • Document revisions with impact notes in chart changelogs.

Chart QA checklist

  • Consistent units and complete time ranges.
  • Clear flags for estimates, interpolation, and breaks in series.
  • Downloadable data plus a citation block on every chart page.
